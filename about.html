About

We all think, I think.

(See what I did there?)

I think a lot. Specifically, I daydream. I take a book, or movie or a real life situation, and I imagine my way through the scenario. Then I do it again, taking a different branch at the last decision point. Then I do it again, at an earlier branch, recursively going through every possible way the scenario could play out.

This site is somewhere I can trim down the fractal daydreams, put them into words, and share some of the (in my opinion) best branches.

I also regularly post less fiction-esque things on Natfan.co.uk, and I'm an Editor for Gaming Masters, a site that does game and tech reviews and discussion.
