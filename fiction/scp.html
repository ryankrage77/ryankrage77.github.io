SCP-3001

Class: Euclid

Containment procedure:
SCP-3001 is supposedly in a standard containment locker in area 17. However, any attempt to certify this is unnecessary. Current containment procedures for the area are sufficient.

Description:
SCP-3001 convinces anyone aware of its existence that it should not be observed, in person, by visual recording or even through description.
People can be made aware of its existence via any method of communicating information, so long as the recipient can understand it.
Description of SCP-3001’s anomalous properties is possible, although it takes considerable willpower.
No researcher is able to open its locker or convince class D staff to do so, as SCP-3001 should remain unobserved. All known anti-memetic procedures should not be implemented, due to the fact that SCP-3001 must remain unobserved.
If SCP-3001 is actually inside its locker (and has no anomalous temporal or dimensional properties), its size may vary from SCP-3001 should not be measured to SCP-3001 should not be measured.

Note: It appears that any attempt to theorize as to SCP-3001’s appearance will result in descriptions similar to the above.

SCP-3001 also causes anyone aware of it to dissuade others from attempting to observe it, as can be seen in this report.

Remember, do not attempt to observe SCP-3001, or try to convince others to do so.
